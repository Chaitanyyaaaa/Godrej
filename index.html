Overview

Godrej Avenue Eleven promises to redefine luxury living. It is a sprawling 3.85-acre freehold land parcel nestled amidst 2.4 acres of serene open spaces and a grand clubhouse. Impeccably designed to offer a lifestyle of unparalleled comfort and convenience, the project boasts a plethora of world-class amenities that cater to the needs of the modern-day urbanite. Godrej Avenue Eleven features an Olympic-length pool, a temperature-controlled pool, and an infinity-edge pool that offers a perfect retreat to relax and rejuvenate. An observatory has been set up for astronomy enthusiasts to explore the wonders of the universe. The grand clubhouse features a lounge, business center, and banquet hall for hosting events and social gatherings. At Godrej Avenue Eleven, meticulously landscaped gardens, a state-of-the-art gym, badminton courts, squash courts, tennis cum multiplay court, and a half-futsal court provide ample opportunities to stay active and fit.

ABOUT GODREJ PROPERTIES

Established in 1897, the Godrej Group has its roots in India's Independence and Swadeshi movement. Our founder, Ardeshir Godrej, a lawyer-turned-serial entrepreneur failed with a few ventures before he struck gold with a locks business. Today, we enjoy the patronage of 1.1 billion consumers globally across consumer goods, real estate, appliances, agriculture and many other businesses. In fact, our geographical footprint extends beyond Earth, with our engines now powering many of India's space missions. With a revenue of over USD 6 billion, we are growing fast, and have exciting, ambitious aspirations.

But for us, it is most important that besides our strong financial performance and innovative, much-loved products, we remain a good company. Approximately 23 per cent of the promoter holding in the Godrej Group is held in trusts that invest in the environment, health and education. We are also bringing together our passion and purpose to make a difference through our Good & Green strategy of 'shared value' to create a more inclusive and greener India.
